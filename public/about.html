About QuickBite

We win on trust

QuickBite is the most reliable way to get great food from local restaurants — delivered fast, accurately and affordably. Customers always know where their order is and what it costs.

Our vision

Reliable food delivery, for a whole neighbourhood

We're building for urban customers who order regularly and value reliability over the widest possible selection. When the experience is dependable, everyone wins — and that starts with the people who make it happen: our restaurant partners and delivery drivers, whose experience directly shapes the customer's.

The problem

Delivery lost the plot on trust

Food delivery is plagued by inaccurate delivery times, opaque fees, payment failures and slow issue resolution. Each one chips away at trust and pushes customers to switch apps. QuickBite exists to fix exactly that.

What we build on

Five principles, one promise

Everything we ship traces back to these pillars.

🛰️

Reliability & transparency

Accurate ETAs, live tracking and clear pricing — you always know where your order is and what it costs.

💳

Frictionless ordering

Ordering and payments that just work, so getting dinner is never the hard part of your day.

🔁

A tight feedback loop

Every complaint is triaged, prioritised and resolved — feedback turns into fixes, fast.

🌱

Healthy supply

Tools that keep restaurants and drivers productive, because dependable supply is what makes delivery reliable.

✨

Personalisation

Help customers reorder favourites and discover food they'll love — without the endless scroll.

🎯

One North Star

Weekly active ordering customers, guarded by on-time delivery rate — reliability is the metric we live by.
